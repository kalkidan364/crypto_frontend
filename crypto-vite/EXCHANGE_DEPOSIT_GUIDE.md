# 🏦 Exchange-to-Exchange Deposit Guide

## 💰 Transfer Crypto from External Exchanges to NEXUS

This feature allows users to deposit cryptocurrency directly from popular exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, and KuCoin into their NEXUS account.

## 🚀 How It Works

### Step 1: Select "From Exchange" Tab
- Go to Deposit page in NEXUS
- Click on "From Exchange" tab (⭐ icon)
- Choose the cryptocurrency you want to transfer

### Step 2: Get Your NEXUS Deposit Address
- System generates a unique deposit address for your selected crypto
- QR code is provided for easy mobile scanning
- Address is specific to your account and the selected cryptocurrency

### Step 3: Follow Exchange-Specific Instructions
Choose your source exchange and follow the detailed step-by-step guide:

## 📋 Supported Source Exchanges

### 🟡 Binance Instructions
1. **Login to Binance** → Open app/website
2. **Go to Wallet → Spot Wallet** → Find your crypto
3. **Click "Withdraw"** → Select crypto withdrawal
4. **Select "Send via Crypto Network"** → Not internal transfer
5. **Paste NEXUS Address** → Copy from NEXUS deposit page
6. **Select Correct Network** → ⚠️ Critical: Match the network
7. **Enter Amount & Confirm** → Complete with 2FA

### 🔵 Coinbase Instructions
1. **Login to Coinbase** → Access your account
2. **Go to Portfolio** → View your crypto holdings
3. **Click on Cryptocurrency** → Select the crypto to send
4. **Click "Send"** → Initiate transfer
5. **Paste NEXUS Address** → In the "To" field
6. **Enter Amount & Send** → Confirm with 2FA

### 🟣 Kraken Instructions
1. **Login to Kraken** → Access account
2. **Go to Funding → Withdraw** → Navigate to withdrawal
3. **Select Cryptocurrency** → Choose crypto to withdraw
4. **Add New Address** → Paste NEXUS address
5. **Verify Address** → Confirm via email
6. **Complete Withdrawal** → Enter amount and confirm

### 🟢 KuCoin Instructions
1. **Login to KuCoin** → Access your account
2. **Go to Assets → Main Account** → Find your crypto
3. **Click "Withdraw"** → Start withdrawal process
4. **Select "On-chain Withdraw"** → Not internal transfer
5. **Add NEXUS Address** → Paste address and select network
6. **Complete Withdrawal** → Verify and confirm

## ⚠️ Critical Safety Information

### 🔴 Network Selection Warning
- **ALWAYS** select the correct blockchain network
- Bitcoin → Bitcoin Network
- Ethereum → Ethereum Network  
- Litecoin → Litecoin Network
- **Wrong network = PERMANENT LOSS OF FUNDS**

### 🟡 Processing Times
- **Exchange Processing:** 5-30 minutes (varies by exchange)
- **Network Confirmations:** 10-60 minutes (depends on network)
- **Total Time:** Usually 15-90 minutes

### 🔵 Minimum Amounts
- **Bitcoin (BTC):** 0.001 BTC minimum
- **Ethereum (ETH):** 0.01 ETH minimum
- **Litecoin (LTC):** 0.01 LTC minimum
- **Bitcoin Cash (BCH):** 0.01 BCH minimum
- **Ripple (XRP):** 10 XRP minimum

## 💡 Pro Tips

### 🧪 Test with Small Amounts First
- Send a small test amount initially
- Verify it arrives correctly
- Then send larger amounts with confidence

### 💰 Consider Fees
- **Exchange Withdrawal Fees:** Set by source exchange
- **Network Fees:** Blockchain transaction fees
- **NEXUS Deposit Fees:** FREE (we don't charge for deposits)

### ⏰ Best Times to Transfer
- **Low Network Congestion:** Early morning or late night
- **Lower Fees:** Weekends often have lower network fees
- **Faster Processing:** Avoid peak trading hours

## 🔍 Tracking Your Deposit

- **Recent Deposits:** Shows in sidebar

- **Transaction History:** Full record of all deposits

### On Blockchain
- **Transaction Hash:** Provided by source exchange
- **Block Explorer:** Track on blockchain explorer
- **Confirmations:** Watch confirmation count increase

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### ❓ Deposit Not Showing Up?
1. **Check Network:** Ensure correct network was used
2. **Wait for Confirmations:** May need 12+ confirmations
3. **Verify Address:** Confirm address was copied correctly
4. **Contact Support:** If still missing after 2 hours

### ❓ Wrong Network Used?
- **Unfortunately:** Funds may be permanently lost
- **Contact Support:** We'll investigate but recovery is rare
- **Prevention:** Always double-check network selection

### ❓ Partial Amount Received?
- **Network Fees:** Deducted from transfer amount
- **Exchange Fees:** May be deducted by source exchange
- **Normal:** Small difference is usually fees
